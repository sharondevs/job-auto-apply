### wikipedia-tell-what
*Task: Go to Wikipedia and tell me what today's featured article is about*

**Required Information:**
- None: No user-provided information is needed to view Wikipedia’s main page.

**Optional Information:**
- None

**Steps:**
1. Navigate to https://www.wikipedia.org/ and choose the desired language (English is usually default for “Today’s Featured Article”).
2. Click through to the language’s main page (e.g., https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Main_Page).
3. Locate the “From today’s featured article” section on the main page.
4. Read the summary provided for today’s featured article.
5. Report back with a concise description of what the article is about, capturing the main subject and any notable details mentioned in the summary.

**Tips:**
- The “Today’s Featured Article” block is typically near the top-left section of the English Wikipedia main page; scroll slightly if you don’t see it immediately.
- No login is required—Wikipedia is openly accessible for viewing featured articles.
