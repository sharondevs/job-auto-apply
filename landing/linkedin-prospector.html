How it works

You tell it who to find. It handles the rest.

1

Picks a strategy

Tell it your goal — networking, sales, hiring, partnerships, or research. It picks the right LinkedIn search approach automatically.

2

Finds your people

Scrolls LinkedIn, reads posts and profiles, and collects personalization hooks — what they posted about, what they're building, what role they just started.

3

You approve the list

Shows you a table with names, roles, and why each person is a match. Nothing gets sent without your say.

4

Sends personalized notes

Every connection note is unique — it references something specific about the person. No templates, no generic messages.

5

Keeps a log

Every person contacted gets logged. Next time you run it, it skips people you've already reached out to.

6

Stops if something's off

Rate limit warning? CAPTCHA? The agent stops immediately and tells you. No risk to your account.
