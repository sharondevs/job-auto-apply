How it works

Strategy first. Replies second.

1

Learns your product

Reads your website or README to understand your value prop, pain points you solve, and what makes you different. Builds a keyword strategy across five layers.

2

Calibrates your voice

Reads your past tweets to learn how you actually talk. Saves a voice profile so every reply sounds like you, not a bot. No em dashes, no "leverage", no LinkedIn-speak.

3

Searches and collects

Searches X for relevant conversations using your keywords. Collects tweets, scores them by relevance, timing, author quality, and reply visibility.

4

Researches each author

Visits every qualified author's profile. Reads their bio, recent tweets, and posting style. Tailors the reply tone to match their energy.

5

You approve the plan

Shows you a table: who they are, their tweet, your draft reply, and why this engagement matters. Nothing gets posted without your say.

6

Engages one by one

Posts each approved reply with natural timing (45-90s gaps). Logs every engagement so it never replies to the same person twice.

Why this is different

Not a reply bot.

Thinks like a marketer

Mixes value-only replies (40%), soft mentions (40%), and direct recommendations (20%). The ratio matters — all product plugs get you flagged as spam.

Sounds like a human

Every reply passes the "typed on my phone" test. No AI giveaways — no em dashes, no "leverage", no perfect parallel structure. Uses your actual voice.

Your real browser

Posts from your actual signed-in Chrome. No API keys, no third-party access. Stops immediately if X shows a rate limit or CAPTCHA.
